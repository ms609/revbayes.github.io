Exercise 2

In exercise 1 we inferred the phylogeny of living bears and estimated relative node ages assuming a global molecular clock model. In this exercise we will relax the assumption of constant rates across the tree and use a relaxed molecular clock model that allows each branch in the tree to have an independent rate.
